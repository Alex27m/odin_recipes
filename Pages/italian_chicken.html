Italian Chicken Marinade

About Recipe

Easy and fast recipe for tasty chicken marinade!

here you will find all ingridients you will need for your first chicken marinade!

Ingridients

  • 1 (16 ounce) bottle Italian-style salad dressing
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps:

    Step 1

  1. Whisk salad dressing, garlic powder, and salt together in a shallow baking dish; add chicken breasts and turn to coat. Cover the dish with plastic wrap and marinate in the refrigerator, 4 hours to overnight.

    2. Step 2

  2. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.

    3. Step 3

  3. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.

    4. Step 4

  4. Remove chicken from marinade and shake off excess; discard remaining marinade.

    5. Step 5

  5. Cook chicken on the preheated grill until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 7 to 8 minutes on each side. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
